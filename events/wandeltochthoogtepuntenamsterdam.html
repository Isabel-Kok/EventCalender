Walk tour highlights Amsterdam

date: 27/01/2022

Enjoy Amsterdam with a professional guide on this walking route. You'll learn about the all day life in the city, see some of the top attractions, and discover local hot spots.

About this activity

  • Free Cancellation
  • Duration 2 hours
  • Tour guide who speaks English

Highlights

Taste real Dutch herring. Stroll through the well-known Flower Market. Taste a pancake or poffertje and learn more about the life of the everyday Amsterdammer. Gathering is at the Royal palace and from there the adventure begins.
