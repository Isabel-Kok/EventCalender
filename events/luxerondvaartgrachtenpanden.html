Luxury canal tour

date: 27/01/2022

Discover the most spectacular sights of Amsterdam along the canals during a luxurious city tour with tourguide. Departure is from the Rijksmuseum. The tour lasts 1.5 hours. Precautions regarding Covid-19. Applicable security measures. You have to keep your distance in the vehicles. The number of visitors is limited to avoid crowds. All contact points are cleaned regularly. Requirements for participants. You must bring and wear your own mouth cap.

About this activity

  • Free Cancellation
  • Duration 1 hours
  • Tour guide who speaks English

Highlights

During this extra luxurious boat trip you can see all the most spectacular sights of Amsterdam from the canals. Along the way you will receive further information from the skipper about what you will see. Cocktails and snacks can be ordered on board. You pass the chic hotel of Amsterdam, the Magere Bridge and the Hermitage, we also sail through the historic canals at the Museum Quarter.
