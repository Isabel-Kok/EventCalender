Bike tour Amsterdam

date: 27/01/2022

If you want to discover Amsterdam in a real Dutch way, take a bike and discover the best locations during this tour.

About this activity

  • Free Cancellation
  • Duration 3 hours
  • Tour guide who speaks English, German and Dutch

Highlights

Tour Amsterdam on the iconic mode of transport by bicycle. Take your bike along all kinds of restaurants in the Jordaan. We also take you along the Pijp and the well-known Red Light District. Take a break at the Vondelpark and enjoy the peace.
