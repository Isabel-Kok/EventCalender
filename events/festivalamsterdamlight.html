Highlights

The 10th anniversary of Amsterdam Light Festival. About twenty light artworks can be seen along the route you get. Precautions regarding Covid-19: Face masks are optional for this activity as the activity takes place outside. The artworks: 1.26 Amsterdam, Bridge of the rainbow, Bunch of tulips, butterfly effect, Darth father, Drawn in light, Ghost Ship, Light a wish, Light lab technasium, Match Girl, Minutes in Blue, Moonburn, MR. J.J van de Veldebrug, Night drawing, Neighborhood, Northern Light, Pas encore mon histoire, Run beyond, Souvenir, Spider, Starry Night, Talking heads, Whole hole, My light is your light.
